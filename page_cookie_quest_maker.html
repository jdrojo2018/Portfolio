Cookie Quest Maker

Project Status Completed
Project Type University / group project
Primary Role(s) Programmer, Story writer
Project Duration ~3 months
Engine and Language Unity, C#

About the project

Narrative-driven, resource management game about baking magical cookie minions and give them tasks to help your town (or not) and its inhabitants.

This project was made in a group of 7, including myself, under the name of S-Pain Games.

As part of the development team, I singlehandely took care of the writing of most dialogs and other minor programming tasks.


Gameplay

Each day, the townsfolk will tell you about the town misadventures. In order to do something about it, you will bake your minions and give them general instructions on how to handle the situation.

To guide your minions on how to proceed, there are 5 different sets of pieces:

  • Hero: The type of cookie minion.
  • Action: The action to do.
  • Target: The entity to act on.
  • Mode: Optional adjective to aid in the task.
  • Object: Optional tool to utilize in the task.

There are 18 main quest to solve, each one has around 3-5 different resolutions depending of given cookie pieces stats.
